How to Load Your Beep Card

Beep cards are essential for a hassle-free commute. Follow these steps to load your card:

  1. Go to any LRT/MRT station and locate the Beep Card loading kiosk.
  2. Insert your Beep Card into the machine.
  3. Select the amount you wish to load.
  4. Insert cash or use a debit/credit card if applicable.
  5. Confirm the transaction and wait for the receipt.

You can also load your Beep Card at convenience stores like 7-Eleven, SM Bills Payment Centers, and select partner establishments.

Loading Options

  • Ticket Booths: Visit any LRT or MRT ticket booth and request a reload. Provide your Beep Card and payment.
  • Ticket Vending Machines: Use self-service ticket vending machines. Insert your Beep Card and follow the instructions.
  • Convenience Stores: Selected convenience stores (e.g., 7-Eleven, Ministop) offer Beep Card loading services.

Online Loading

Some banks and mobile wallets allow you to reload your Beep Card through their apps. Follow their instructions to complete the process.

Check Your Balance

You can check your balance at ticket booths, vending machines, or via mobile apps that support Beep Card services.
