The LRT Line 2 offers affordable rates for all commuters. Here are the fare details:

Regular Fare Rates

  • Minimum Fare: Php 13
  • Maximum Fare: Php 28

Discounted Fares

The following groups are eligible for a 20% discount:

  • Senior Citizens
  • Persons with Disabilities (PWD)
  • Students (with valid ID)

How Fares Are Calculated

Fares are determined based on the distance traveled. Shorter trips will cost the minimum fare, while longer trips will approach the maximum fare.
