Important Contact Numbers

If you experience an emergency while using LRT Line 2, please contact the following:

  • LRT Line 2 Security: (02) 8533-3811
  • LRT Authority (LRTA): (02) 647-2350
  • Philippine National Police (PNP): 117 or (02) 8722-0650
  • MMDA Hotline: 136
  • Red Cross Emergency Hotline: 143
  • National Disaster Risk Reduction: (02) 8911-1406
  • Fire Department: 160
  • Ambulance Services: 911

What to Do in an Emergency

Follow these steps if you encounter an emergency:

  • Remain calm and alert authorities immediately.
  • Listen to announcements and follow instructions from staff.
  • Locate emergency exits and use them safely.
  • Assist those in need, especially children and the elderly.
