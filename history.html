Discover the milestones of the LRT Line 2 system.

  • 1997: Construction begins on LRT Line 2.
  • 2003: The first segment (Santolan to Araneta Center-Cubao) opens.
  • 2004: Full operations begin, extending to Recto.
  • 2021: The Antipolo extension opens, adding two new stations.
  • Present: Plans for further expansion are being explored.

ðŸš† Fun Facts

  • LRT Line 2 is the widest train system in Manila, making it more spacious than LRT-1 or MRT-3.
  • It was designed to handle up to 600,000 passengers per day.
  • The line runs on driverless train technology but still has operators for safety.
