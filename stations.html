LRT Line 2 Stations

The LRT Line 2 (Blue Line) operates from Antipolo to Recto with 12 stations. Do note that the one with the Purple Line is now outdated. Below is a list of the stations and brief information about each:

1. Antipolo Station

The easternmost station of LRT Line 2, located in Antipolo City. It serves as a major hub for commuters from Rizal Province.

2. Marikina-Pasig Station

Located between Marikina and Pasig, this station provides access to key residential and commercial areas in both cities.

3. Santolan Station

A busy station near Santolan Road, known for its proximity to several schools and shopping centers.

4. Katipunan Station

This station serves the Katipunan Avenue area, with easy access to educational institutions like Ateneo de Manila University and Miriam College.

5. Anonas Station

Located in Quezon City, the Anonas Station serves residential and commercial districts along Anonas Street.

6. Araneta Center-Cubao Station

A major transfer point for passengers connecting to LRT Line 1 and the MRT-3. Itâ€™s also adjacent to the Araneta City complex.

7. Betty Go-Belmonte Station

This station is located near New Manila in Quezon City, named after Betty Go-Belmonte, a notable journalist and publisher.

8. Gilmore Station

A station popular for its proximity to IT shops and electronics stores along Gilmore Avenue.

9. J. Ruiz Station

Named after J. Ruiz Street, this station serves nearby residential areas and schools in San Juan City.

10. V. Mapa Station

Located near V. Mapa High School, this station is a convenient stop for students and residents in the area.

11. Pureza Station

A station known for its accessibility to nearby universities and colleges in Manila.

12. Legarda Station

Situated near several universities and landmarks like the University of the East and the San Sebastian Basilica.
